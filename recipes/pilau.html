Pilau is an aromatic samptuous meal made with rice and potatoes cooked with either beaf of chicken broth.It is known to have originated from india and is mostly enjoyed in the coastal parts of Kenya.

Additionally, a variety of spices are used to give it it's fragmatic and spicy nature, ranging from cumin seeds,black pepper to cloves et al.

ingredients

  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil(60ml)
  • 2 medium red onions,thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic,minced
  • 2 tablespoons ginger,minced
  • 1 serrano chile,finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon pilau masala
  • 2 beef stock cubes
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro(10g),roughly chopped
  • 1 lb beef sirloin(455g),cubed
  • roma tomatoes,diced
  • 1 lb potato(455g),waxy,peeled and cut into1 inch(2cm)chunks
  • 4 cups water(960ml)
  • 2 cups basmati rice(400g)

Preparation

  1. heat pot medium heat then add oil,saute and red onions. let them cook for 10-15 min until they are dark brown.
  2. Add cilantro,cinnamon stick,pilau masala,beef stock cubes, ginger,garlic ad serrano chile and let it cook for 1-2 min until aromatic.
  3. Add the beef and cook until browned for about 8 minutes.
  4. Add the tomatoes and let them cook until the have released their juice for 4-5 minutes.
  5. Add potatoes nad water and let it cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Add rice,stir and cover then reduce the heat level to medium low and let it cook for 20 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and the liquid is fully absorbed.
  7. The food is ready for serving
